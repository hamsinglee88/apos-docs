# 扫码购


## 1、创建使用扫码购

如果想在商城内使用扫码购，需要在应用商店内开通团购应用，开通入口在系统页面右上角->应用商店->商店首页内找到“扫码购”。

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F7c191aea-3a37-473a-8b82-d7700816f6d3_image.png?Expires=1807424982&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=f5yUP76Oc9R2Qn2xJ2hsqTGsQ%2BU%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

选中扫码购应用，点击进入![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2Fe4dc5cdd-c127-4ed6-a9b3-360e4899d4e0_image.png?Expires=1807425647&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=2n38JXJuTd0Brq1WbAf6wV5%2FIC0%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击进入扫码购应用

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2Faf2997eb-013f-473a-9fcc-aed474dd26f6_image.png?Expires=1807426288&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=tmzg%2BVOpm85Dc7gbAWRPT6w%2BN3E%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

扫码购应用中点击“商城扫码购按钮”，会出现微信小程序的二维码，扫码即可进入此商户商城

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F8653a536-c8b8-4e7b-9503-afc266579ec5_image.png?Expires=1807426052&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=UGlFQenZzqe81KCuyJ8UNaBQKJw%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击导购右侧 +添加 按钮，弹出一个添加导购弹窗，弹窗中显示此商户中所有的员工，员工显示顺序按照 运营管理--部门管理--员工 新增的员工先后时间展示顺序

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2Fcc6ae4b2-2078-4886-bcd5-f92cd16d2287_image.png?Expires=1807426447&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=xRevRXELFKX1Jh%2F24bdQpbbBbsU%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

弹窗中选中员工，点击确定 保存，扫码购应用中会显示已经添加的员工为导购。

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F422a92f3-b202-48fb-9ace-a55537180c77_image.png?Expires=1807426677&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=Z23jYg4RJSb5u8zL%2FfNc7Sxxs%2BM%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

## 2、进入商城使用扫码购

进入商城，点击我的页面--我的服务--扫码购应用，点击进入即可
![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2Feb06dc4a-54e9-4389-b4ff-dccaf3b6c07a_image.png?Expires=1807427324&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=ou%2Bkql51yaQ1ybFHHeCysvV%2FqBE%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击扫码购应用进入，显示扫码购应用首页

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F2e834634-9b47-45cd-8099-c0bd063e62c1_image.png?Expires=1807427304&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=Uuf6YUl1cJJo5NDIK2loNH2%2FD0k%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击 选择导购 按钮，弹出选择导购弹窗，弹窗中显示可选择的导购员，可以任选一位为导购员

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F05c38811-d169-456b-8e0b-299acda9da39_image.png?Expires=1807427596&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=TBBdCoxyaw8gdK8IuCum0IgDz9M%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击扫码购中，扫商品条码按钮，出现扫一扫，去扫商品条码

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F9a975328-e162-4a29-92fc-9c2e5b0bdb38_image.png?Expires=1807428073&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=j9CfnC0tL6PZgIHeu26reuo29h8%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

点击购物车按钮，跳转至购物车页面并显示已经添加成功至购物车的商品

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F15331ab6-68ac-4dff-b036-5b2561d31597_image.png?Expires=1807428959&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=Ah1BWJ5wyExxJoa91vPkuuT28oA%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

通过扫码购扫一扫，成功添加商品至购物车（扫码购首页点击购物车按钮，直接跳转购物车页面）

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F177bb914-e6e0-4bbc-a842-dc5aa1d7194b_image.png?Expires=1807428375&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=W%2Fh6XdZwPo5AXuU4kC0M3ulMK1I%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

如果商品扫码扫不出来，点击输入条形码按钮，跳转至手动输入条码页面

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F185cb937-8e71-44fd-84db-6e8a76fc4f02_image.png?Expires=1807429091&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=90DdnTXssXYuEem6Nh%2FzB8TxX0I%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

当输入的商品条码错误时，会合理提示商品条码不正确或商品未录入

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F26312074-8458-48ba-8f5a-8a48a2e0eaca_image.png?Expires=1807429320&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=WuL48yx6%2F0DmuUMTyw1cyF76jEM%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)


手动输入商品条形码，添加成功时，跳转购物车页面，购物车中添加最新的商品，

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F2d315a6f-933b-4457-943a-c18fe175be86_image.png?Expires=1807429176&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=u2CNRV7LS9Ks3mRqWhb3cKGC1NM%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

返回购物车页面时，点击扫一扫按钮可以继续扫码商品添加添加至购物车

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F947197b1-8bd0-4ab1-adbc-ab652193c31e_image.png?Expires=1807429531&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=ACZ%2FDki7dxbtNY4BRTOQ7sXXwBE%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2Ff9d24a27-b3d9-41dd-892c-c5e0c61339c5_image.png?Expires=1807429678&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=rHnvebq8UP%2BpLiDnhLAYV9CuYbk%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)

![image.png](http://media-hk01.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/images%2Fjackli%40jieztech.com%2F%E6%89%AB%E7%A0%81%E8%B4%AD.md%2F3cef0801-b1f7-4678-a914-6b15ed2512c3_image.png?Expires=1807429714&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAIXyAB35iFpHM0&Signature=drdf3xtEo8L5lQTYZwDxP11Hv%2Bk%3D&x-oss-process=image%2Fformat%2Cpng)
